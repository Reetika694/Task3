Welcome to Patna - The Heart of Bihar

Patna, the capital of Bihar, is one of the oldest inhabited cities in the world. Situated along the banks of the holy river Ganga, the city is rich in culture, history, and tradition. It has played a vital role in the development of India, from the times of ancient kingdoms to the modern day.

The iconic Golghar, peaceful Ganga Ghats, and spiritual places like Buddha Smriti Park attract thousands of tourists every year. Patna is not just about heritage; it is also a growing urban center with top educational institutions and infrastructural development.
